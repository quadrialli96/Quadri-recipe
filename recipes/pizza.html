Pizza

A tomato pizza with red peppers, jalapeno, green pepper and chicken toppings.

INGREDIENTS
  • Yeast
  • Dried basil
  • Water
  • Jalapeno
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh Oregano
  • Sliced black olives
  • Bread flour
  • Chicken breast
  • Cornmeal
  • Green peppers
STEPS
  1. Start with a crust. A wide range of pizza crust mixes, pre-made crusts and refrigerated doughs are available.
  2. Add a sauce. Go light on the sauce to avoid a soggy rust
  3. Add some veggies
  4. Add fruits
  5. Add some protein
  6. Add cheese
  7. Bake your creation in ahot oven(450 F or above)
